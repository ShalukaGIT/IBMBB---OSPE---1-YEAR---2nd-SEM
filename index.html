OSPE Flashcards Guide

  • Getting Started: Select a specific category (e.g., Microscopy, Calculations) or choose 'All Categories' for a mixed review.
  • Flipping Cards: Tap directly on any flashcard to flip it and reveal the detailed answer and rationale.
  • Progress Tracking: After flipping a card, use the 'Got it' (green) or 'Study again' (red) buttons to track your mastery. Your score is displayed at the bottom!
  • Navigation: Use the 'Next' and 'Prev' buttons to cycle through cards, or use the 'Jump to' input at the bottom to skip to a specific question number.
  • Images & Zoom: Many cards include real microscopic images or lab setups from your practicals. Study them carefully for the spot exam.
